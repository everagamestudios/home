Privacy Policy

Please review our privacy policy to understand how Evera Game Studios handles information in our games.

Effective Date: 14th April 2026

We're glad you enjoyed playing our game. Now, we want to take a moment to talk about something really important: your privacy. We care about keeping your information safe, and we've put together this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect, use, and protect your data when you're playing our game.

1. Information We Collect

We don't gather any personal info that could identify you, like your name, email, or contact details.

However, we may collect non-personal information automatically, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system, language)
  • Gameplay data (progress, session duration, interactions)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • Crash logs and performance data

2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Improve gameplay experience and performance
  • Analyze user behavior and engagement
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Provide and optimize advertisements

3. Third-Party Apps

Our game works with other companies that might collect information about you, and they have their own rules about how they handle that information.

  • Google AdMob (ads)
  • Unity Ads (ads)
  • IronSource (ads mediation)
  • Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)

When you use these services, they might gather and process certain information from your device, like what kind of device you're using, how you're using the service, and how you interact with ads.

It's a good idea to take a look at the privacy policies of these companies to learn more about how they handle your personal info.

4. Data Sharing

We keep your personal information safe and don't share it with anyone else.

We may share non-personal data with trusted third-party services for:

  • Analytics
  • Advertising
  • App performance monitoring

5. Children’s Privacy

This game is for people who are 13 years old or older.

We don't collect personal info from kids under 13 on purpose. If you think a child has given us their personal info, let us know and we'll delete it right away.

6. Data Security

We do everything we can to keep the information we collect safe from people who shouldn't have it, and to prevent it from getting lost, used in the wrong way, or changed by mistake.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data, including:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to request deletion of data
  • The right to opt out of personalized ads (via device settings)

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We might make some changes to this Privacy Policy now and then. If we do, we'll let you know by updating the date on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you've got questions or worries about our Privacy Policy, just get in touch with us at:

Email:everagamestudios@gmail.com
